About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, born on 5th November 1988 in Delhi, India, is one of the greatest batsmen in cricket history. Known as the "Chase Master", he has redefined consistency and aggression in modern-day cricket. Former captain of the Indian national team, Kohli has numerous records to his name across all formats of the game.
